The Career Cat was founded by a specialist who spent over a decade working inside South Africa's most demanding industries — as a recruiter, HR professional, and advisor to both job seekers and the companies hiring them.

We know how CVs get discarded before a human ever reads them. We've watched automated systems reject strong candidates for minor formatting issues. We've seen outstanding people undersell themselves in interviews because no one had ever coached them on what interviewers actually want to hear.

The founder prefers to stay behind the curtain — not because they're hiding anything, but because this service is about you, not us. Our expertise speaks through the results we deliver.

We serve everyone from first-time job seekers to senior executives, from blue-collar workers looking for their next placement to managers navigating a career pivot. Every client gets the same sharp, honest, bespoke approach.
